About Me

The Story
Behind the Work

Hello! I'm Kleinhugh Rotciv R. Ocharon, a 2nd year BSIT student at Bohol Island State University – Balilihan Campus with a strong foundation in system diagnostics, troubleshooting, and hardware optimization.

Technology has always been part of my everyday life, especially when it comes to solving simple computer or internet problems for friends and family. Through these hands-on experiences, I became more interested in computers, troubleshooting, and networking and eventually, design and animation.

My creative vision is to build systems and visuals that are simple, functional, and helpful for people and organizationsbdeveloping solutions that make tasks easier, faster, and more organized.
